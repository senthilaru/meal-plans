# 2-Week Tamil Nadu Style Meal Plan (Traditional, Healthy, Balanced)

This plan focuses on authentic **Tamil Nadu home-style cooking**, incorporating healthy fibers, proteins, and daily probiotics.

*   **Pattern:**
    *   **Morning (Tiffin):** Traditional light breakfast.
    *   **Lunch (Saapadu):** Rice-based with Kuzhambu, Poriyal, Kootu, and Rasam.
    *   **Evening (4:30 PM):** **Health Soups & Light Homemade Snacks**.
    *   **Dinner:** Light tiffin items (avoiding heavy rice).

---

## WEEK 1

### Day 1
*   **Breakfast:** **Kanchipuram Idli** (Spiced with pepper, ginger, cumin) + **Malli (Coriander) Thogayal**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Paruppu Urundai Kuzhambu** (Lentil balls gravy - High Protein) + **Vazhaipoo (Banana Flower) Poriyal** (High Fiber) + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Murungai Keerai (Drumstick Leaves) Soup** + **Kadalai Mittai** (Peanut Chikki - 1 piece).
*   **Dinner:** **Kuthiraivali (Barnyard Millet) Pongal** + **Gotsu**.

### Day 2
*   **Breakfast:** **Adai** (Lentil Pancake) + **Avial** (Mixed Veg).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Sundakkai Vatha Kuzhambu** (Turkey Berry) + **Chow Chow Kootu** + **Appalam** + **Neer Mor**.
*   **Evening:** **Thakkali (Tomato) Soup** (Chettinad style with spices) + **Roasted Pori (Puffed Rice)** with peanuts.
*   **Dinner:** **Idiappam** + **Thengai Paal** (Sweet Coconut Milk - light) or Vegetable Kurma.

### Day 3
*   **Breakfast:** **Ragi Puttu** + **Kadalai Curry** (Black Chickpeas).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Keerai Masiyal** (Spinach mash) + **Urulai Roast** (Potato) + **Rasam** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Vazhaithandu (Banana Stem) Soup** (Kidney health) + **Masala Sundal** (Boiled Peanuts/Peas).
*   **Dinner:** **Godhuma (Wheat) Rava Upma** + **Thengai Chutney**.

### Day 4
*   **Breakfast:** **Ven Pongal** + **Brinjal Gotsu** (Chidambaram style).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Arachuvitta Sambar** (Radish/Drumstick) + **Kothavarangai (Cluster Beans) Poriyal** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Kollu (Horsegram) Soup** (Weight loss/Heat) + **Ellu Urundai** (Sesame Ball).
*   **Dinner:** **Kal Dosa** (Soft sponge dosa) + **Vada Curry** or **Thakkali Thokku**.

### Day 5
*   **Breakfast:** **Pesarattu** (Green Gram Dosa) + **Inji Puli** (Ginger Tamarind Dip).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Mor Kuzhambu** (Ash gourd/Pumpkin) + **Vendakkai (Lady's Finger) Curry** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Mixed Vegetable Clear Soup** + **Pattani (Dried Peas) Sundal**.
*   **Dinner:** **Oats Idli** + **Kara Chutney**.

### Day 6
*   **Breakfast:** **Varagu (Kodo Millet) Arisi Upma** + **Chutney**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Ennai Kathirikai Kuzhambu** + **Pudalangai (Snake Gourd) Kootu** + **Rasam** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Milagu (Pepper) Rasam/Soup** + **Vella Seedai** (Homemade sweet snack - small portion).
*   **Dinner:** **Appam** + **Veg Stew** (Coconut milk based).

### Day 7
*   **Breakfast:** **Poori** + **Kilangu (Potato Masala)**.
*   **Lunch:** **Veg Biryani** (Seeraga Samba rice) + **Onion Raita** + **Bread Halwa** (Small treat).
*   **Evening:** **Lemon Coriander Soup** + **Bajji** (Raw Banana - occasional treat) or **Roasted Gram (Pottukadalai)**.
*   **Dinner:** **Semiya Upma** + **Coconut Chutney**.

---

## WEEK 2

### Day 8
*   **Breakfast:** **Kuzhi Paniyaram** (Karupatti/Sweet or Kara/Spiced) + **Kaara Chutney**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Manathakkali Vatha Kuzhambu** + **Paruppu Usili** (Beans/Cluster beans steamed with lentils) + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Mudakathan Keerai Soup** (Good for joints) + **Kala Chana (Black Channa) Sundal**.
*   **Dinner:** **Ragi Dosa** + **Peanut Chutney**.

### Day 9
*   **Breakfast:** **Samai (Little Millet) Pongal** + **Sambar**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Thakkali Rasam** + **Senai Kizhangu (Yam) Varuval** + **Keerai Poriyal** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Carrot & Ginger Soup** + **Ribbon Pakoda** (Homemade, small portion).
*   **Dinner:** **Chappathi** + **Green Gram Dal**.

### Day 10
*   **Breakfast:** **Sevail** (Lemon/Coconut) + **Potato Chips** (Homemade).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Mulai Kattiya Payaru (Sprouted Green Gram) Kuzhambu** + **Vazhakkai Podimas** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Beetroot Soup** + **Mochai (Field Beans) Sundal**.
*   **Dinner:** **Broken Wheat Upma** + **Pickle**.

### Day 11
*   **Breakfast:** **Vegetable Rava Kichadi** + **Coconut Chutney**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Poondhu (Garlic) Kuzhambu** + **Sorakkai (Bottle Gourd) Kootu** + **Appalam** + **Buttermilk**.
*   **Evening:** **Nellikai (Gooseberry) Soup/Juice** + **Thattai** (Homemade crisp).
*   **Dinner:** **Dosa** + **Podalangai Paal Curry**.

### Day 12
*   **Breakfast:** **Aapam** + **Kadala Curry**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Jeera Rasam** + **Peerkangai (Ridge Gourd) Poriyal** + **Thuvayal** (Paruppu/Thengai) + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Sweet Corn Veg Soup** + **Boiled Peanuts**.
*   **Dinner:** **Rava Dosa** + **Sambar**.

### Day 13
*   **Breakfast:** **Samba Ravai Upma** + **Sugar/Chutney**.
*   **Lunch:** **Arisi Paruppu Sadham** (Coimbatore special) + **Vadam** + **Ghee** + **Curd**.
*   **Evening:** **Ash Gourd (Poosanikai) Soup** + **Karamani (Cowpea) Sundal**.
*   **Dinner:** **Idli** + **Milagai Podi** (Gunpowder) + **Oil/Ghee**.

### Day 14
*   **Breakfast:** **Thinai (Foxtail Millet) Sweet Pongal** (Special) + **Medu Vada**.
*   **Lunch:** **Bisibelebath** + **Potato Chips/Kara Boondi** + **Curd Rice** + **Pickle**.
*   **Evening:** **Karuveppilai (Curry Leaf) Soup** + **Omapodi**.
*   **Dinner:** **Fruits & Milk** or **Kozhukattai** (Steamed dumplings).

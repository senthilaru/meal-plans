# 2-Week South Indian Meal Plan (High Protein, Fiber, Balanced)

This meal plan is designed for adults and kids, focusing on:
*   **Morning & Night (Meals 1 & 3):** Light, easy to digest, rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins.
*   **Afternoon (Meal 2):** Balanced meal with main carbs, proteins (dal/legumes), vegetables, and probiotics.
*   **Daily Probiotics:** Curd (Yogurt) or Buttermilk is included in every Lunch to ensure gut health.

## General Guidelines
*   **Cooking Oil:** Use Gingelly oil (Sesame) or Coconut oil for authentic taste and health.
*   **Probiotics:** Homemade Curd or Spiced Buttermilk (Neer Mor) must be part of Lunch.
*   **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
*   **Kids:** Reduce spice levels for kids; add a dollop of ghee for good fats.

---

## WEEK 1

### Day 1
*   **Breakfast:** **Pesarattu (Green Moong Dosa)** (High Protein) + Ginger Chutney.
    *   *Nutrients:* Moong dal provides high protein and fiber. ginger aids digestion.
*   **Lunch:** Steamed Rice/Millet + **Spinach (Keerai) Masiyal** (Fiber/Iron) + **Lady's Finger Fry (Vendakkai Poriyal)** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Millet Idli** (Fiber) + **Mixed Vegetable Sambar** (Protein/Vitamins).

### Day 2
*   **Breakfast:** **Ragi Puttu** (High Calcium/Fiber) + Kadala Curry (Black Chickpeas - High Protein).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Drumstick Sambar** + **Carrot & Beans Poriyal** + **Spiced Buttermilk**.
*   **Dinner:** **Adai** (Mixed Lentil Dosa - High Protein) + Avial (Mixed Vegetables - Vitamins).

### Day 3
*   **Breakfast:** **Ven Pongal** (Lentil & Rice) + **Gotsu** (Eggplant/Veggie mash).
    *   *Note:* Use less ghee/more dal ratio for adults for lighter option.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Vatha Kuzhambu** (Berry gravy) + **Cabbage Poriyal** + **Roasted Papad** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Wheat Rava Upma** with plenty of peas and carrots (Fiber) + Coconut Chutney.

### Day 4
*   **Breakfast:** **Idli** + **Tiffin Sambar** (load it with veggies) + **Mint Chutney** (Vitamins).
*   **Lunch:** Rice/Millet + **Dal Tadka** + **Snake Gourd (Pudalangai) Kootu** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Appam** + **Vegetable Stew** (Coconut milk + Carrots/Potatoes/Peas - Light & Vitamin rich).

### Day 5
*   **Breakfast:** **Kanjeevaram Idli** (Spiced Idli) + **Peanut Chutney** (Protein).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Buttermilk Kuzhambu (Mor Kuzhambu)** + **Potato Roast** (Kids favorite) + **Cucumber Salad**.
*   **Dinner:** **Godhuma (Wheat) Dosa** + **Tomato Onion Thokku**.
    *   *Side:* Boiled **Green Gram Sundal** (High Protein snack/side).

### Day 6
*   **Breakfast:** **Vermicelli Upma** (add lots of veggies) + Coconut Chutney.
    *   *Tip:* Use roasted vermicelli and minimize oil.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Lemon Rasam** + **Raw Banana (Vazhakkai) Varuval** + **Broad Beans Poriyal** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Uttapam** (topped with onions/tomatoes/carrots) + Sambar.

### Day 7
*   **Breakfast:** **Set Dosa** (Soft) + **Vegetable Sagun/Kurma** (High fiber/vitamins).
*   **Lunch:** **Vegetable Biryani** (Mild) + **Onion Raita** (Probiotic) + **Boiled Egg / Paneer Fry** (Protein boost).
*   **Dinner:** **Idiappam** (String Hoppers) + **Thengai Paal** (Coconut Milk, light) or Veg Kurma.

---

## WEEK 2

### Day 8
*   **Breakfast:** **Oats Idli** (High Fiber) + **Tomato Chutney**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Beetroot Sambar** (Vitamins) + **Plantain Stem (Vazhaithandu) Kootu** (High Fiber/Detox) + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Chapati** (Whole Wheat) + **Green Gram Dal (Dal Fry)** (High Protein).

### Day 9
*   **Breakfast:** **Kuzhi Paniyaram** (using leftover batter, add carrots/onions) + **Kaara Chutney**.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Pepper Rasam** (Good for immunity) + **Yam (Senai Kizhangu) Roast** + **Chow Chow Kootu** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Ragi Dosa** (Calcium/Fiber) + **Peanut & Garlic Chutney**.

### Day 10
*   **Breakfast:** **Broken Wheat (Samba Ravai) Upma** + Pickle/Chutney.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Arachuvitta Sambar** (Traditional mixed veg) + **Cluster Beans (Kothavarangai) Poriyal** (Fiber) + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Mini Idli** soaked in Sambar (Sambar Idli) - easy to digest + **Pomegranate Salad**.

### Day 11
*   **Breakfast:** **Poori** (Occasional treat) + **Potato Masala** with Green Peas.
    *   *Alternative for Adults:* Methi (Fenugreek) Thepla/Chapati.
*   **Lunch:** **Curd Rice** (Probiotic Main) + **Mango Pickle** + **Sundal** (Chickpea Salad - Protein) + **Roasted Morel (Mor Milagai)**.
*   **Dinner:** **Neer Dosa** (Very light) + **Tomato Kurma**.

### Day 12
*   **Breakfast:** **Lemon Sevai** (Rice noodles) + Coconut Chutney.
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Ennai Kathirikai Kuzhambu** (Brinjal gravy) + **Ridge Gourd Poriyal** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Dosa** + **Egg Curry** or **Paneer Butter Masala** (Protein focus).

### Day 13
*   **Breakfast:** **Aval Upma (Poha)** with Peanuts and Peas (Light & Protein rich).
*   **Lunch:** Rice + **Mysore Rasam** + **Cauliflower Roast** + **Keerai (Greens) Kootu** + **Curd**.
*   **Dinner:** **Vegetable Rava Dosa** + **Coconut Chutney**.

### Day 14
*   **Breakfast:** **Pongal** (Harvest style with veggies if desired, or classic) + **Sambar**.
*   **Lunch:** **Variety Rice** (Tomato Rice or Tamarind Rice) + **Vadam/Papad** + **Chickpea Sundal** (to balance protein) + **Buttermilk**.
*   **Dinner:** **Fruit Salad & Nuts** with lightweight **Idli (2 pcs)** or just **Warm Milk** if very light dinner is preferred. Alternatively: **Upma Kozhukattai** (Steamed Rice dumplings).
